FAQ

Are my images uploaded to a server?
No. All processing happens locally in your browser via the Canvas API. Nothing is uploaded to any server.
What formats are supported?
This tool supports JPG/JPEG, PNG and WEBP image formats for conversion between the four offered converters. Some browsers may have limited WebP encoding support; modern browsers work fine.
How is image quality controlled?
JPEG and WebP quality sliders allow you to balance file size and visual quality. PNG exports are lossless by default.
Can I convert multiple images at once?
You can drop multiple files into a dropzone; each will be converted individually and provided as downloads.
